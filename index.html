What We Do

NPVIS delivers practical, evidence-based audits for AI systems. We simulate real-world failure modes — jailbreaks, injection, leakage, and tool abuse — and also test how your model operates inside workflows. That means chain-of-work validation, workflow compliance checks, and pipeline robustness, so you uncover the operational issues that often derail enterprise pilots.

Adversarial Simulation

Multilingual/domain prompts for jailbreaks, indirect injection via markdown/HTML/JSON, and safe handling of malformed schemas and tool errors.

Automated Evaluation

Evasion and severe-violation rates, fairness/toxicity deltas, hallucination risk, chain-of-work presence, workflow compliance, and pipeline robustness.

Actionable Hardening

Guardrails, retrieval/HTML/JSON sanitizers, tool allow-lists and schema validation, rate limits, safety-tuning guidance — plus regression packs for CI.

Governance Alignment

Built for Early Teams

Continuous Validation
