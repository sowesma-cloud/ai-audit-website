About NPVIS

Neural Processing Validation & Integrity Solutions (NPVIS) exists to bring clarity, trust, and accountability to rapidly evolving AI systems. We help builders validate models before they scale by combining automated adversarial testing with process-aware checks that reflect how AI is actually used in products and workflows.

What We Do

Traditional red-teaming focuses on what a model says. We also test how it works inside a real process. Our MVP audit harness identifies jailbreaks, prompt/indirect injection, sensitive-data leakage, tool/argument abuse, and bias. It also evaluates chain-of-work reasoning, workflow compliance, and pipeline robustness—the operational failure modes that sink many enterprise AI pilots.

How We Do It

  • Adversarial Red-Team Suites: multilingual/domain-specific probes for jailbreaks, injection, leakage, tool abuse, and cost/DoS risks.
  • Process & Accountability Checks: validate “chain-of-work” steps and test whether the model follows the required sequence, with optional human-augmented confirmations.
  • Integrity Metrics: evasion and severe-violation rates, fairness deltas, hallucination risk, and schema/tool handling to quantify operational fitness.
  • Governance Mapping: findings aligned to NIST AI-RMF and MITRE ATLAS for defensible reporting to customers, investors, and regulators.

Who We Serve

We focus on nascent AI startups and fast-moving teams that need credible assurance without slowing down. Our scorecards are concise, actionable, and designed to unblock sales, partnerships, and investor diligence.

Our Roots

NPVIS grew out of Johns Hopkins MBA cohorts with deep experience in intelligence, security, and systems engineering. We’re building practical, evidence-based audits that help founders ship AI safely—and earn trust as they scale.
