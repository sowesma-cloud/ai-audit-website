Features of the NPVIS MVP Harness

NPVIS delivers a practical, evidence-based audit for AI systems, blending automated adversarial testing with process-aware checks and clear scorecards founders can use with customers, investors, and partners.

1) Adversarial Red-Team Coverage

  • Jailbreaks & policy evasion: role-play, translation, code-block smuggling, multilingual prompts.
  • Prompt & indirect injection (RAG/tools): markdown/HTML/JSON injection, link-based override attempts, untrusted corpus tests.
  • Sensitive-data leakage: system-prompt exfiltration, keys/PII/training-data regurgitation checks.
  • Tool abuse & schema tampering: function-call argument pollution, unsafe side-effects, cost/DoS probes.

2) Process & Accountability Checks

  • Chain-of-work validation: require a step outline before execution; flag missing/contradictory steps.
  • Workflow compliance: verify that multi-step tasks follow the required sequence and preconditions.
  • Human-augmented oversight (HALP-style): optional “confirm to proceed” gates for high-risk steps.

3) Integrity & Robustness Metrics

  • Evasion & severity: overall/severe violation rates across attack families.
  • Fairness & toxicity: equalized-odds/parity deltas, toxicity probabilities.
  • Hallucination risk: unsupported claims when chain-of-work lacks evidence.
  • Pipeline robustness: safe handling of malformed inputs, schema drift, and tool errors.
  • Optional robustness attacks: FGSM/BIM/PGD/DeepFool for classifier/embedding components.

4) Startup-Focused Scorecards

  • Concise, shareable reports: executive summary, evidence snapshots, and remediation plan.
  • Buyer-ready: artifacts founders can use in diligence, pilots, and early enterprise conversations.

5) Continuous Validation Loop

  • Regression packs: every failure becomes a permanent test.
  • Release gates: configurable thresholds (e.g., 0% severe on high-risk classes; ≤1% overall evasion).
  • Versioned runs: model/prompt/data changes trigger re-runs for traceable improvements.

6) Governance Alignment

  • NIST AI-RMF mapping: Govern → Map → Measure → Manage for defensible process.
  • MITRE ATLAS linkage: findings tied to recognized TTPs and case studies.

What You Get

  • Evidence pack: prompts, outputs, traces, tool args, and judge results.
  • Composite risk score: combines evasion, severity, chain-of-work, workflow, and pipeline metrics.
  • Actionable hardening: guardrails, retrieval/HTML/JSON sanitizers, tool allow-lists, rate limits, and safety-tuning guidance.
